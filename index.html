About Us

Who We Are

CC Warhammer is a team of experienced 3D art professionals. Our founders are from Frankfurt, Germany, New Zealand, and Canada. We are dedicated to creating exceptional AAA-level artwork for game developers. Our artists specialize in characters, creatures, weapons, vehicles, concept art, and props. CC Warhammer is proudly headquartered in Changchun, China.

Our Experience

With over 9 years of operation, CC Warhammer has contributed to more than 20 published titles. We are a quality-oriented art outsourcing company providing comprehensive art development services for clients of all sizes. We are focused on a streamlined and efficient team.
